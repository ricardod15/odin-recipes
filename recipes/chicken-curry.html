Chicken Curry

Description

Chicken curry is a savoury dish found in many South Asian cuisines, prepared by cooking chicken pieces in a sauce made from onions, tomatoes, garlic, ginger, and a combination of spices. The flavours can range from mild and aromatic to hot and intensely spiced depending on regional variations. The curry is characterized by tender, juicy chicken pieces immersed in a rich, flavorful gravy, often served with rice, roti, or naan, making it a common staple for everyday meals and special occasions alike.

Ingredients

  • 300 g chicken pieces (bone-in or boneless)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped or 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • ½ tsp red chili powder (optional)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • ½ cup water
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil and add cumin seeds until aromatic.
  2. Sauté onions until translucent.
  3. Stir in turmeric, coriander powder, and salt.
  4. Add turmeric, coriander powder, chili powder, and salt; stir briefly.
  5. Add tomato and cook until softened.
  6. Add chicken and water; cover and simmer for 12 to 15 minutes, until chicken is cooked.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve with steamed rice or roti.
